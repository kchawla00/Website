Hobbies

Swimming

I enjoy being active and staying fit. Swimming gives me both of those. I have been swimming since I was 4 and joined my first swim team in the summer of 2017. Since then I have been on many competitive swim teams. Even when I'm not at practice, I want to go in the water to have fun!

Baking

In my free time I love to bake different desserts. I like to bake brownies, cookies, and tons more. I also like to make some Indian sweets like barfi and kaju katli. My favorite part is watching my friends and family eat my desserts and get their rating on how they like it.

Dancing

Over anything else my favorite thing to do is dance. It doesn't matter what type of song it is or if it is an Indian song or an American song, I just get up and start to dance. I also take Bollywood dance lessons and perform in recitals every now and then.

Art

Another way I express myself is by doing art. I like to sketch, paint, and make some Indian art. At first I hated to paint. All the mess it made, and the fact that once it's on, it's hard to get off, really wasn't my thing. After a lot of practicing I started to like it more and now I actually love to paint. A little hard work and dedication really goes a long way!
