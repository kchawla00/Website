My Experience

I love traveling and it is so fun to try new foods and see new places.

Morroco: In Morroco we went on a camel and we slept in the Sahara Desert!!! We also had so many mint teas and stayed in so many different and cool places.

Mexico: I've been to Mexico many times, but I mostly go to Cancun. We went to Cancun for our friends 50th birthday party and it was so FUN!!

Taj Mahal: I went to the Taj Mahal with my family and it was so fun!!. It was so beautiful and I loved all the details.

Statue of Liberty: The Statue of Liberty was so cool and so big. It was so cool going to the pedestal and seeing the view of New York!!
