About

Kavya Chawla


Hi I am Kavya Chawla and I am 11 years old. I live in Chicago, Illinois and I'm in middle school. My favortie subjects in school are math and writing. I've always loved looking up at space but my liking for it grew when my dad bought me a telescope. Aside from liking space, I swim competitively and I am also creative. I dance, play an instrument and create art. I also enjoy baking desserts like banana bread, cakes, brownies and cookies.

I have many activities during the day and I have found that with my practice of yoga I can stay focused on what I am doing in that moment. In the future, I see myself teaching yoga. I like to volunteer at an organization that supports feeding starving children around the world. It makes me feel good and it helps the children that need it.

I enjoy all of my activities, but the most fun part is making new friends and hanging out with them. I play sports with my friends at school and at swim practices. I look forward to coming home to my family after school. I love watching movies and TV shows with my family at night and playing games with my brother. I am grateful for everything I have and for my friends and family.
